Team comparison visualization
This visualization helps to compare the individual teams' strengths and weaknesses in order to create well-balanced teams.
The following tour explains the main aspects of the visualization. Normally, it is opened side-by-side with the assignment screen on a very wide screen or dual-screen setup.
The visualiuation consists of five different rows--one for each aspect of the self-assessment data. The columns of each row show the teams' respective values. Each skillset is shown separately for male and female players.
Note that the visualization updates automatically as you change the assignments of players on the main screen.
